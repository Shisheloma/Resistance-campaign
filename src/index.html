Who we are

Resistance Movement is the soil of anarchist, globalist, moralist, anti-evil and anti-authoritarian initiatives.

We believe that planet Earth* and all Eastern, Western, Asian and American Europe should be free from dictatorship, totalitarianism and all life manifestations we found, specify and consider as evil. Freedom, solidarity and equality should become the main principles of the social, classial, conscious and unconscious organization of life.

Our task: to unite efforts of fighters against all mentioned above for the sake of effective struggle for our ideals and values. We aspire to influence the future of our movement and the whole humanity, to protect the freedoms which already exist and to contribute to their extension.
